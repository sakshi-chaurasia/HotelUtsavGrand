ABOUT THE UTSAV GRAND

WHO WE ARE

The Utsav Grand Sonipat is first Luxery hotel in the city, which is known for giving complete solution to the corporate and social events. It exclusively follows the ancient tradition and also welcomes evolutionary modren variation. The facade and the interior of the hotel have been tastefully done up in order to combine ethnicity and modernity.

The emergence of a landmark, elegant hotel spread out in 3 acres of pristine locale, amid verdant green spaces, in the NCR township of Sonipat, just off the National Highway-1, conveniently and centrally located for easy accessibility yet far from the madding crowd. Quintessentially Indian in its fabric The Utsav Grand is rooted in its local heritage.

Basking in the luxury of plush deluxe rooms or relaxing in the lap of nature in the cool green lawns, you can plan out a perfect day to visit the historical sites of Delhi, Panipat or Kurukshetra or just curl up with your laptop to work away from the breakneck pace of corporate offices. Or simply relax, away from it all- spend some quality time with family, friends or just by yourself and feel the stress of the urban lifestyle melt away in the aesthetic ambience of its interiors or ensconced in the lap of nature.

WHY GUEST CHOOSE US?


Get back in form in our state-of-the-art health club and fitness centre equipped with all the latest apparatus your body craves and indulge yourself in the welcoming comforts and hospitality that this amazing resort has to offer.

And if it is a wedding that you need to plan or a conference to be arranged then avail any of the three opulent banquet halls- Darbar , Satkaar and Jalsa – spreading out into the emerald swathes of velvety turf in the party lawns-Anjuman and harit.

The accommodation option at Utsav combine historical legacy with contemporary features and amenities who stay in suites. with a total of 35 rooms Utsav can accommodate their guests with their exclusive and premium services.

For a fine-dining experience, step into Aahar, our five star restaurant with a vibrant culinary culture and gratify your taste buds with the delights offered in a variety of well researched authentic cuisine- a blend of Indian and Continental, contemporary Chinese and exotic oriental fare from the far east.

Savour our hospitality – a blend of the traditional and modernity - ideal for a discerning clientele.
